## The 7 beats

**1 · Open on the phone (30s)** — launch from the home-screen icon. "This is our academy. Built by us, for the land team." Sign-in screen → you're already in.

**2 · Home (1 min)** — the hero: *"One question changes how you show up."* Point at "For your goal · Regenerative Steward": "The AI curates honestly — and if it's wrong, you tell it" (tap *"Not seeing what you need?"* to flash the tutor). Answer the **Daily Drop** live: "one question a day keeps the whole team's streak alive."

**3 · Play the real lesson (1.5 min)** — Land Team Journey → Module 1, play 20–30s of the film. Then (pre-completed account) show **"What you take with you"** — "every module ends with the three things that matter. Nobody else's LMS does this moment."

**4 · Flip to Portuguese (30s)** — one tap: EN → PT. Everything: courses, quizzes, tutor. "Half our team lives in Portuguese. So does the platform."

**5 · AI (2 min)** — Voice search: 🎤 *"I'm looking for something about soil"*. Then the tutor: ask *"quiz me on above the line"* — real AI, real content. Then the kicker (laptop, Admin → **AI Course Studio**): paste any transcript → *"Write the course ✦"* → full bilingual course draft in ~30s. **The line: "Filming the lesson is the only manual step."**

**6 · Community (30s)** — pinned welcome, real posts, @mentions. "Every journey has its own conversation."

**7 · Close: the Leader's Cockpit (2 min)** — laptop, Admin. Real members, completion, streaks, at-risk flags. Click **Nudge** on your own account → **the email arrives on the phone on the table.** "Learn, measure, re-engage — one loop. Completion is the metric; this is the machine that moves it." Then deck slide 8 (roadmap): Department Digests as the vision.

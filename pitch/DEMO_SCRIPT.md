- [ ] Publish the latest `firestore.rules` — one click, unlocks missions, assignments, live schedule, reactions, polls, team AI key

## The 10 beats

**1 · Open on the phone (30s)** — launch from the home-screen icon. "This is our academy. Built by us, for the land team." You're already in; the first-run tour will have greeted new members before you.

**2 · Home (1 min)** — the hero: *"One question changes how you show up."* Show the **assignment card** ("📌 Assigned to you — the Studio put it there") and answer the **Daily Drop** live.

**3 · Ask the Academy (1 min)** — the golden ask bar. Type: *"How do I slow erosion on the slope path?"* — AI answers **from our own courses**, with cards linking to the exact lesson. Tap one. "This is Sana's flagship enterprise feature. It runs on our free stack."

**4 · Play the real lesson + takeaways (1.5 min)** — Land Team Journey → Module 1, 20–30s of the film. Then the takeaways moment, the ⭐ rating, and **⤓ Download certificate** — show the branded certificate PNG. "Completion you can hold — and add to LinkedIn."

**5 · Field Mission (1 min)** — scroll the same course page: *"Catch yourself below the line — +150 XP."* Submit is note + photo. "Every other platform ends at the quiz. Ours ends on the land — a leader approves the proof."

**6 · The Practice Arena (1.5 min)** — same page: 🎭 practice "The blame spiral" with Rui. Send two replies, then **End & get feedback** — the scored rubric appears. "Rehearse the hard conversation before it happens. Nobody at any price ships this."

**7 · Flip to Portuguese (30s)** — one tap: EN → PT. Courses, quizzes, missions, the coach — everything. "Half our team lives in Portuguese. So does the platform."

**8 · Journeys + Community (1 min)** — Paths → **The Land Steward Journey**: the milestone map, mission stages, capstone, journey certificate. Then Community: presence dots, the seeded poll (vote live), reactions, the official welcome broadcast.

**9 · The Studio (2 min, laptop)** — Admin. Content tab: open any course, edit the Portuguese title, **Save & publish** — "your ops team edits the whole academy like a Google Doc." Then AI Course Studio: paste a transcript → full bilingual course in ~30s. **"Filming the lesson is the only manual step."**

**10 · Close: the Cockpit (2 min)** — People tab: real members, the **skills heatmap**, **compliance** ("fire safety recertifies itself — look, one expired"), mission review queue, then click **✦ AI weekly digest** — the AI writes your leadership brief live. Click **Nudge** on your own account → **the email arrives on the phone on the table.** Then deck slide: roadmap. "Learn, measure, re-engage, prove it on the land — one loop."

## If asked…
- **"Is this real data?"** — Yes, all of it: accounts, progress, streaks, leaderboard, cockpit, certificates, emails. Nothing in Analytics is invented.
- **"What does it cost to run?"** — Hosting ~€0 (GitHub Pages + Firebase free tier + Vimeo). AI: free on Gemini's tier, or cents/day.
- **"What about privacy?"** — EU data residency, consent per channel, self-serve export + delete on every profile.
- **"Can other organisations use it?"** — That's the vision slide: white-label regenerative-learning platform.

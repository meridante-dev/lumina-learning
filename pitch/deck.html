The problem

Field teams don't learn from portals.

Corporate LMS platforms were built for desks: logins people forget, SCORM files nobody finishes, dashboards nobody reads. Meanwhile the knowledge that matters — how we treat the soil, the water, and each other — lives in people's heads and leaves when they do.

  • 01Completion is the metric that matters — and industry averages are dismal.
  • 02Deskless teams need learning that meets them on a phone, in their language, in minutes.
  • 03Making courses is the bottleneck — filming is easy; structuring, translating and assessing is weeks of work.
